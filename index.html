Learn About Us

Non-profit community-centric organization organization

  • Objectives
  • Mission
  • Vision
  • To advance knowledge and implementation of science- and technology-based initiatives in urban and rural communities of Cameroon, and Chester County, PA, USA.
  • To coordinate with local authorities and promote understanding of the benefits and risks of modern technology through outreach programs in urban and rural communities of Cameroon, and in Chester County, PA, USA.
  • To help the poor, underprivileged, and handicapped persons in urban and rural communities of Cameroon and Chester County, PA, USA, through education, dissemination of information and provision of needed resources to improve their living conditions.
  • To promote high educational standards, economic growth, and career development opportunities for ordinary people in urban and rural communities of Cameroon, and in Chester County, PA, USA.
NNACOR strives to offer science- and technology-based initiatives, out-reach programs and meaningful solutions to address problems associated with under-development, community deterioration, disease, unmet medical needs, poverty, and lack of good education, knowledge, and economic opportunities that ordinary people encounter in urban and rural communities of Cameroon, and in Chester County, PA, USA.
NNACOR is a non-profit, community-centric organization, that is dedicated to improving the living conditions of ordinary people in urban and rural communities of Cameroon, and in Chester County, PA, USA. Our strategy is to empower ordinary people and their community through education, and sharing of scientific and technological knowledge that would lead to creation of economic and development opportunities, improved health and well-being, and foster the common good in urban and rural communities of Cameroon, and in Chester County, PA, USA.
